Our Story

Passion for
Perfect Fit

Nethra Fashion was born from a single belief — every woman deserves to feel extraordinary in what she wears. Founded in 2010, we have grown into one of the most trusted boutique tailors in the city.

  • Master tailors with 20+ years of experience in ethnic and western wear
  • Premium fabrics sourced from the finest mills across India
  • Precise measurements and multiple fitting sessions for perfection
  • On-time delivery guaranteed — especially for weddings and occasions
  • Designs inspired by the latest runway trends, customised for you
